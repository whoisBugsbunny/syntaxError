SYNTAXERROR is a group of students from Lovely Professional University who are passionate about solving the problems faced by students in rural areas. We believe that every student deserves access to quality education, regardless of their background or financial situation. We are developing a system to enhance the quality of education in rural areas by addressing the following challenges:
  • Financial issues: We are providing a platform where students can learn for free or at a subsidized cost.
  • Internet issues: We are providing students with the ability to download lectures for offline viewing.
  • Biased scholarship system: We are providing the government with an education score that they can use to select students for scholarships based on their merit, rather than caste or gender. Our system will also provide the following benefits to students in rural areas:
  • Access to high-quality educational content: We will provide students with access to a wide range of high-quality educational content from top educators and institutions.
  • Personalized learning experience: Our system will use artificial intelligence to personalize the learning experience for each student based on their individual needs and interests.
  • Supportive community: Our system will provide students with access to a supportive community of learners and mentors.
We believe that our system can have a significant impact on the lives of students in rural areas by helping them to get the education they need to succeed. We are committed to working with the Government of Kerala to implement our system and make it available to all students in rural areas. Our Team Members:
